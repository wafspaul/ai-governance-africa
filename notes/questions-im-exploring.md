## Big questions (the ones I keep coming back to)

1. **Is job displacement actually a systemic risk, or just a serious harm?**
   What's the difference? The AI Safety Atlas defines systemic risks as risks to the overall system — not just harm to individuals. At what scale does job displacement become systemic? Is there a threshold? This matters because it changes what kind of governance response is needed.

2. **Who benefits from AI automation in Africa, and who loses?**
   This is a distribution question. It's possible that AI creates new jobs even as it destroys old ones. But the people who lose jobs and the people who gain them may not be the same people. Understanding this distribution matters enormously.

3. **How do informal economies respond to AI-driven displacement?**
   Kenya's informal sector employs a huge share of workers. If formal BPO jobs disappear, do workers just move into informal work? Does that make the problem smaller or just less visible?
