## Rules for the whole 30 days

1. **Write first, AI second.** Draft every important piece yourself. Use AI to edit, not to think.
2. **Commit something every week.** Visible progress is proof of seriousness.
3. **Stay in your lane.** You are not a technical AI alignment researcher. You are an African policy/governance researcher asking: what are the systemic risks of AI-driven labour disruption? Own that.
4. **Show your uncertainty.** "I don't know yet, but here are the questions I'm asking" is stronger than fake confidence.
5. **Don't wait until it's perfect.** Publish the rough essay. Post the imperfect LinkedIn post. Progress beats polish at this stage.
