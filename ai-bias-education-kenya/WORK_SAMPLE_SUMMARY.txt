## AI Governance Work Sample

**Title:** Education as a Proxy Variable in AI-Driven Income Prediction  
**Context:** Kenya labor and education data  
**Focus Area:** Algorithmic fairness, proxy discrimination, governance risk

### Research Question
Does educational attainment function as a proxy for gender-based income inequality in data commonly used to train AI systems?

### Key Insight
The analysis shows persistent income disparities between men and women at similar education levels, indicating that education may encode structural inequality rather than neutral merit.

### Governance Relevance
AI systems trained on such data risk reproducing inequality unless governance mechanisms such as fairness audits, proxy analysis, and regulatory oversight are applied.

### Methods
Exploratory data analysis, group comparison, visualization, governance interpretation.

### Outputs
- Reproducible Jupyter notebook
- Visual evidence of disparity
- Governance-focused interpretation
